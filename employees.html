Our Team

Angela: Owner

Angela has been with the Durham House of Pets for over 30 years when she and her husband John opened the shop together. She graduated with a degree in Animal Science and loves sharing her knowledge with others. She once spent over a month in Southeast Asia where she fell in love with elephants.
