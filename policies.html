Policies

Financing

  • 1.Purchases discounted upon using cash in store
  • 2.Purchase now and pay later on purchases over $300
  • 3.10% off when you repurchase any dog or cat food

Delivery

Delivery is available for any location within a 100 mile radius of Durham House of Pets. This excludes the shipping of live animals. Shipping options include Next Day, 2 Day, and Standard shipping. Pricing will vary depending on the shipping option chosen. Please call the front desk to place any delivery orders. On line ordering coming soon!

Return Policies

In store returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase. A proof of purchase is required with all returns in order to receive a full refund. Items that appear to be used extensively will not be accepted. If items delivered appear damaged upon arrival or you simply placed the wrong order, contact us and we will send you a return label to send your item(s) back for a refund or simply exchange for the right product.
